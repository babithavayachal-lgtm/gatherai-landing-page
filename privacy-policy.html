Your Privacy Matters to Us

Smart Homework Solutions ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register and use our Service, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, Google account details (when signing in with Google)
  • Profile Picture: From your Google account (if you sign in with Google)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Razorpay or Stripe (we do not store card details)
  • Contact Information: Email address for communications and support

1.2 Educational Content

  • Homework Questions: Text and images of homework questions you submit
  • Grade and Subject Information: Selected grade level and subjects
  • Question History: Record of all questions asked and answers provided
  • Progress Data: Subject-wise analytics and learning patterns
  • Report Cards: Exam scores and grades you input

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, error logs
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and functionality

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Store your question history for future reference
  • Provide progress tracking and analytics
  • Maintain digital report cards
  • Personalize your learning experience

2.2 Account Management

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Process subscription payments
  • Send important account notifications
  • Provide customer support

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Train and improve AI accuracy
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Fix technical issues and bugs

2.4 Communications

  • Send service-related emails (subscription confirmations, payment receipts)

4.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4.2 Security Measures
